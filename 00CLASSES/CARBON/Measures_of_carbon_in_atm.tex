Measures of carbon in the atmosphere

\begin{enumerate}
  \item Concentration(s) of \ce{CO2} is usually reported as ppmv (parts per million by volume, mole fraction)
  \item In order to convert to mass fraction ppmm (parts per million by mass) multiply by $\frac{44.0095}{28.97} = 1.519$ (44.0095 molar mass of \ce{CO2}, 28.97 molar mass of the atmosphere).
  \item To determine mass of \ce{CO2} in the atmosphere multiply mass fraction by mass of atmosphere $5.1480 \times 10^{18}$ kg.
  \item Partial pressure of \ce{CO2} is equal to mass fraction.
  \item Mass of carbon in the atmosphere is the mass of \ce{CO2} multiplied by $\frac{28.97}{44.0095} = 0.2729$
\end{enumerate}
